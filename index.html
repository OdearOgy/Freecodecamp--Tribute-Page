Todd Howard's Life Journey

  • 1971 - Born in Lower Macungie Township, Pennsylvania
  • 1989 - Graduated from Emmaus High School in Emmaus, Pennsylvania
  • 1993 - Graduated form The College of William & Mary in Wlliamsburg, Virginia
  • 1994 - Joined Bethesda Softworks
  • 1995 - Producer and Designer of The Terminator: Future Shock and Skynet
  • 1996 - Designer of The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall
  • 1998 - Project Leader and Designer of The Elder Scroolls Adventures: Redguard
  • 2002 - Project Leader and Designer of The Elder Scroll III: Morrowind
  • 2006 - Executive Producer of The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion
  • 2008 - Game Director and Executive Producer of Fallout 3
  • 2011 - Game Director and Executive Producer of The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim
  • 2012 - Was named "Best Game Director" by Academy of Interactive Arts and Sciences.
  • 2014 - He received the Lara of Honor, Germany's lifetime achievement award for gaming.
  • 2015 - Game Director and Executive Producer of Fallout 4
